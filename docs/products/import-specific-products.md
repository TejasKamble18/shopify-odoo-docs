# Import Specific Products

Import one or more specific products by their Shopify Product ID.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Shopify → Products → Import Specific Products**
2. Select your **Shopify Instance**
3. Enter one or more **Shopify Product IDs** (comma-separated)
4. Click **Import**

## Finding Shopify Product IDs

In Shopify Admin:
1. Go to **Products**
2. Click a product
3. The ID is in the URL: `shopify.com/admin/products/**7890123456**`
