# Export Shipment Tracking

Push tracking numbers from Odoo delivery orders back to Shopify to mark orders as fulfilled.

## Steps

1. Validate the delivery order in Odoo
2. Add the **Tracking Number** and **Carrier**
3. Navigate to **Shopify → Orders → Export Shipment**, or click **Send to Shopify** on the delivery order

Shopify marks the order as **Fulfilled** and sends the tracking email to the customer.
