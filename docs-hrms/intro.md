## What is the HRMS Dashboard?

The SDLC Corp HRMS Dashboard is a production-ready Odoo module that centralises all your HR operations — from employee onboarding and payroll to leave management and performance reviews — in one unified dashboard inside Odoo.

## Key Capabilities

| Category | What's Included |
|---|---|
| **Employees** | Employee profiles, onboarding workflows, org chart |
| **Payroll** | Salary structures, payslips, tax computation |
| **Leave & Attendance** | Leave requests, approvals, attendance tracking |
| **Performance** | Appraisals, KPIs, goals tracking |
| **Reports & Analytics** | HR dashboards, headcount reports, payroll summaries |

## Supported Versions

- **Odoo**: v18, v19
